Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 30, 2025

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, such as when you create an account, update your profile, log workouts, or upload progress photos. This may include your name, email address, physical characteristics, and historical workout data.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to provide, maintain, and improve our services, including to:

  • Track your workout progress and streak statistics.
  • Personalize your fitness plans based on your target muscles and equipment.
  • Send technical notices, updates, and support messages.

3. Data Storage

Your data, including progress photos and metrics, is stored securely. We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties except as required by law or as described explicitly in this policy.

4. Cookies

We use cookies and session storage technologies to maintain secure user login states and keep track of selected settings (such as active workout plans and hydration logs).

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions or feedback regarding this Privacy Policy, please reach out to us via the contact form on our contact page.
