Getting Started

1. Start a Workout: Go to the Home screen and select a preset (Beginner, Intermediate) or build a Custom plan.

2. Track Progress: Use the "Progress" tab (Body icon) to log weight and measurements.

3. Streaks: Consistency is key! Workout daily to build your streak.

FAQ

How is calorie burn calculated?

We use MET values (Metabolic Equivalent of Task) multiplied by your body weight and duration.

Can I use this offline?

Currently, FlexZone requires an internet connection to save your progress.
