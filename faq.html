Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about FlexZone

Is FlexZone free to use?
Yes! FlexZone is built on a free tier model that allows you to track your workouts, view history, and use custom workout features for free without any hidden costs.
How do I create a custom workout?
Navigate to the Home page and click "Custom" under Quick Actions. There, you can input your target muscle group, duration, and the equipment you have available to instantly generate a tailored routine.
Can I export my data?
Yes, you fully own your fitness data. Go to your Profile page and select "Export Data" from the sidebar. A CSV containing your complete workout log history will download immediately.
How do I change my profile picture?
On your Profile page, click the camera icon on your avatar. Choose any image file from your device, and it will automatically crop and upload as your new profile picture.
