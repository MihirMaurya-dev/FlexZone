Frequently Asked Questions

Is FlexZone free to use? ▼
Yes! FlexZone allows you to track your workouts, view history, and use basic features for free.
How do I create a custom workout? ▼
Navigate to the Home page and select "Custom" from the Quick Actions menu. You can then specify muscle groups, duration, and equipment.
Can I export my data? ▼
Yes, go to your Profile page, scroll to the Settings section, and click "Export Workout History" to download a CSV file of your logs.
How do I change my profile picture? ▼
On your Profile page, click the camera icon next to your avatar to upload a new photo.
